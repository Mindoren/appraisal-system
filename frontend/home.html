Staff Performance Appraisal System (SPAS)

The Staff Performance Appraisal System (SPAS) is a digital platform developed to facilitate employee performance management within Uasin Gishu County Government. The system supports work planning, KPI tracking, quarterly reviews, mid-year reviews, annual appraisals, performance monitoring and report generation.
